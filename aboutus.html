Who We Are

We are a company with more than 10 years of experience. Founded by Camila Apai in 2010 in the Carlsbad, California area. Camila started this venture on her own, guided by her love for healthy food and her passion for creating organic fruit drinks. As time passed and the business began to expand, more people joined the Bountiful Foods team.

We currently have a dynamic team, made up of five members who work in our physical store. In turn, we have several food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. Both in our physical store and food trucks, we are recognized for our efficiency and the quality of our products. For this very reason, the public knows us for being able to offer them the best options on the market and excellent service.

Our Expectations

Our mission is to provide healthy food options to the public, helping to support healthy eating behavior and organic bounty.
